SAM models are downloaded automatically on first use into the `models/sam/` folder at the project root (created on demand). You can also pre-download them yourself and place the .pt files there to avoid downloads.

Target directory: <project_root>/models/sam/

Download from: https://docs.ultralytics.com/models/sam-2/

Direct download links:
Tiny:  https://github.com/ultralytics/assets/releases/download/v8.2.0/sam2_t.pt
Small: https://github.com/ultralytics/assets/releases/download/v8.2.0/sam2_s.pt
Base:  https://github.com/ultralytics/assets/releases/download/v8.2.0/sam2_b.pt
Large: https://github.com/ultralytics/assets/releases/download/v8.2.0/sam2_l.pt

SAM 2.1 variants are available from the same Ultralytics release page (sam2.1_t.pt, sam2.1_s.pt, sam2.1_b.pt, sam2.1_l.pt).

Be cautious with the large model — it demands higher computing and memory resources.
